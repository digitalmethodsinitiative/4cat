What is {{ __tool_name }}?

{{ __tool_name }} is a software suite for capture and analysis of thread-based platforms, created and run by OILab at the University of Amsterdam as part of the ERC-funded ODYCCEUS project.

It is currently under development. At this time, you can use it to query select boards of 4chan, Reddit and 8chan. We welcome feedback: mail us at 4cat@oilab.eu.

Updates

  1. 27 August 2019 The interface for creating datasets has been updated and now additionally allows you to upload your own CSV files for processing.
  2. 6 August 2019 4CAT was inaccesible for a few days because of some TLS issues. Apologies for the inconvenience.
  3. 19 June 2019 We are aware of some issues with our 8chan search where queries return no results when they ostensibly should. We hope to have a fix for this out soon &em; our apologies for the inconvenience.
  4. 2 May 2019 Reddit is now available as a data source capture and analysis. This data is sourced from the Pushshift API.
  5. 6 Feb 2019 Result pages now include a preview of the posts found, so you can get a quick impression of what your query returned.
  6. 29 Jan 2019 We've upgraded our server hardware and rebuilt the search indexes; the full corpus of posts is now once again available.
  7. 18 Jan 2019 Added 8chan as a data source. The boards pol, leftypol, v and brit will be scraped.
  8. 11 Jan 2019 Added the lgbt board to the 4chan scraper.
